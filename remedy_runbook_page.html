Remedy & ITSM Workflow Design

Incident, change, and problem flows that keep regulators satisfied and engineers focused on solving real issues.

Incident Management

Incidents are triaged with clear categorization, impact, and urgency fields that map directly to automation triggers. The goal is to reduce noise while preserving a complete audit trail.

  • Standardized templates for recurring incident types.
  • Embedded runbook links and automation entry points.
  • Post‑incident review fields aligned with SRE best practices.

Change Management

Change windows are treated as first‑class citizens—just like in the Infrastructure Initiative simulator. Each change has a defined scope, risk rating, and rollback plan.

  • Pre‑approved change models for low‑risk, high‑frequency tasks.
  • Automated notifications to stakeholders and on‑call rotations.
  • Integration points for Ansible playbooks and CI/CD pipelines.

Problem Management

Root cause analysis is documented in a way that is searchable, reusable, and automation‑friendly. The goal is to turn every major incident into a future automation opportunity.
